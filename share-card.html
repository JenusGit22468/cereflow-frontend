Stroke Recognition

F.A.S.T

Every Second Counts in Stroke Detection

Time is brain - Act immediately if you see these signs
F
Face Drooping
Does one side of the face droop or is it numb?
Ask the person to smile. Is their smile uneven or lopsided?
A
Arm Weakness
Is one arm weak or numb?
Ask the person to raise both arms. Does one arm drift downward?
S
Speech Difficulty
Is speech slurred or strange?
Ask them to repeat a simple phrase. Are their words slurred or confused?
T
Time to Call
If you see any of these signs, time is critical.
Call emergency services immediately and note the time symptoms first appeared.
Emergency Services
Call your local emergency number immediately
Common numbers: 911 (US/CA), 999 (UK), 112 (EU), 000 (AU), 119 (JP)
