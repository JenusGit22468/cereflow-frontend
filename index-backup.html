Rapid Response Critical

For every 10-minute delay in treatment within the first 3 hours, 1 in 100 patients loses a chance at improved recovery.

Door-to-Needle Target

National target: Treatment within 60 minutes of hospital arrival. Currently achieved in only 18.3% of golden hour patients.

Recognition Protocol

Know F.A.S.T. Save Lives.

F

Face Drooping

Does one side of the face droop? Ask them to smile.

A

Arm Weakness

Is one arm weak or numb? Ask them to raise both arms.

S

Speech Difficulty

Is speech slurred? Are they unable to speak clearly?

T

Time to Call

If any symptoms, call emergency services immediately.
